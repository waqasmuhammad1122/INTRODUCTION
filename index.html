Introduction

Located at the University of Campania, Italy, our group specializes in the intersection of Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, and Computational Medicinal Chemistry. We are dedicated to developing novel ML algorithms for GPCR engineering to enhance thermostability and pioneering new antitrypanosomal agents.

By integrating molecular dynamics simulations, CADD, and dry-lab transcriptomics, our final goal is to leverage computational models to improve the clinical development of tailored therapeutics.
