About Our Research

Located at the University of Campania, Italy, our group specializes in the intersection of Bioinformatics, Machine Learning, and Computational Medicinal Chemistry. We integrate molecular dynamics simulations, CADD, and dry-lab transcriptomics to improve the clinical development of tailored therapeutics.

Figure 1: Outline of our computational pipeline.

  • Developing Machine Learning algorithms for GPCR engineering to enhance thermostability.
  • Identification of constrained peptidomimetics as antitrypanosomal agents.
  • Molecular Dynamics Simulations and Computer-Aided Drug Design (CADD).
